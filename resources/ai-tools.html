Free AI Tools for Teachers

Enhance your teaching with these AI-powered tools. From generating content to providing feedback, these resources can save time and expand your educational capabilities.

Large Language Models

While not exhaustive, below is a selection of some of the best AI assistants currently available. They broadly offer similar functionality, but each has its own quirks and pros and cons, so it’s worth experimenting. If you’re using free versions, you may find the rate limits restrictive, so trying a few models might be worthwhile.
