Welcome

This page is for pupils in Years 7–8 (ages 11–13). Read these short tips so you know what’s OK and what’s not.

  • You won’t be using generative AI in school.
  • Microsoft Copilot is for 13+ (not for Years 7–8).
  • Most other AI tools are 16+ or 18+.
  • If you use AI at home, do it with a parent/carer’s permission and supervision.

Key Do’s and Don’ts

Keep it simple. Be safe, kind and honest online.

Do
  • Ask a trusted adult before using any AI tool at home.
  • Keep personal info private (full name, phone, address, school details, photos, location).
  • Be kind and respectful online.
  • Check facts with reliable sources or a teacher.
Don’t
  • Share personal data, photos, or login details with AI tools.
  • Believe everything AI says — always double‑check.
  • Use AI to do your work for you.
  • If you get help with ideas at home, do your own writing and follow your teacher’s rules.
  • Upload schoolwork, class photos, or anything private to websites without permission.
Remember

Our school behaviour policy still applies when using technology — be honest, kind and safe.

You’re all set

Thanks for reading. Keep these tips in mind at school and at home.

If you’re ever unsure, ask a teacher or a trusted adult.
